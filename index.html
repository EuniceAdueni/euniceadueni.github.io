Educational background

  • 2014

    Baccalaureate

    I obtained a science baccalaureate at the Lycée Moderne de Grand-Bassam in Côte d'Ivoire. Through Scholarship in 2014, I began a new adventure as a foreign student in Morocco where I studied mathemactics and computer science at the university of Ibn Zohr.

  • 2017

    Bachelor degree in Mathematics and Comptuer Science

    After 3 years of study, I successfully got my bachelor degree in mathematics and computer sciences. Automatically, I looked forward to pursue in master. Fortunately, I have been accepted in many university and engineering school such as INSEA (Morocco), ENSIAS (Morocco) and Aix-Marseille (France). After a deep reflection, I have decided to live Morocco for university of Aix-Marseille, Marseille, France. This choice has been driven by several opportunities that the university as well as the country can offer to students.

  • 2019

    Master degree in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

    Excited by this new journey, I started my first year of master degree in computer science with enthusiasm and joy. For the second year, I decided to specialize in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I found myself passionate about this field. Immersed in this world of neural networks and automatic learning; I obtained my master degree. As if it wasn't enough, I decided to deepen my knowledge by started a new course. The city of Nancy was my new destination.

  • 2020

    Master degree in Natural Language Processing

    In this new master, I was like a child in front of sweets. It was multicolor experience. Many people from several places in the world were my classmates. I had to understand their culture and work with them as a team. Moreover, the language was a barrier as it was my first time ever to study in english. All these difficulties make me strong enough to overcome the challenges and successfully obtain my master degree. At the end, I'm thankful and proud to achieve my dreams because I have gotten two masters degree (one in Natural Language Processing and the second in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning).

My professional experiences

Enedis (ex ERDF)

April 2019 - September 2019
The internship was mainly about text classification with the aim of improving the automation of tasks. During it, an automatic routing of customer complaints by Deep learning was made. This gave the way to the creation of a web portal for interaction between the neural classification model and the user. Another task was to analyse the "work orders" and make a classification of interventions.

Laboratoire Informatique d'Avignon (LIA)

February 2020 - August 2020
The internship subject is distributed learning for speech regognition in the context of privacy protection. The work assigned is to implement in pyTorch an algorithm for automatic speech recognition with neural models and to make an experimental protocol for measuring performance. Futhermore, to draw a content analysis of the exchanges between acoustic models during learning.
